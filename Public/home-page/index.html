GUIDELINES ON THE USE OF MULTIMEDIA EQUIPMENT AND FACILITIES

Ensure responsible and efficient use of multimedia equipment and facilities by adhering to the established guidelines.

1. Advance Reservation Requirement

The borrower shall personally contact the staff member at least 24 hours before the scheduled classroom activity. For Department / University-wide activities, a three-day advanced reservation is required.

2. Equipment Request Form and Approval

The borrower should accomplish the Application of TLTD/TLT Section Equipment, Materials, Services Form. Staff will inform if request is not approved due to schedule conflicts or equipment unavailability.

3. Return Policy and Fines

All borrowed equipment must be returned after use. No overnight borrowing allowed except with Security Department endorsement (7:00 AM return). PHP 100.00/hour fine for late returns.

4. Instructional Materials Catalogue

A catalogue shall be made available for the choice of instructional materials.

5. Preview and Editing Room Access

Preview, editing, or dubbing of materials requires advanced reservation for Preview and Editing Room access.

6. Off-Campus Equipment Use

Equipment taken off-campus requires VP approval letter through Head/Coordinator, submitted three days in advance.

7. Personal Equipment and Materials Clearance

Faculty and students must secure entry/exit clearance from Security Office for personal media equipment.

8. Liability for Damaged or Lost Equipment

Borrower is responsible for intentional damage or loss of borrowed equipment and materials.
